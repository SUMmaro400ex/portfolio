Cloud Appointments
Cloud Appointments

Cloud Appointments is a telemedecine application connected to the CareCloud api. This was built during a 24hour period as part of a hackathon competition. A patient can schedule their next cloud appointment through the app. The doctor's office will then see the appointment on their schedule. When it's time for the appointment, the doctor will click the link embedded in the appointment to start the video call. The patient will then receive a text message that the doctor is ready with a link to join.
